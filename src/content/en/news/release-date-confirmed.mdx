## Official Release Date Announcement

Pearl Abyss has officially announced that **Crimson Desert** will launch worldwide. After years of anticipation following its reveal, the open-world action RPG set on the continent of Pywel is finally coming to players around the globe.

The game follows **Kliff**, leader of the Greymane mercenaries, as he battles the Black Bears and uncovers the truth behind the supernatural force known as the **Abyss**. With a deep action combat system, massive open world, and a story spanning 60+ hours, Crimson Desert is positioned to be one of the most ambitious RPGs of the year.

## Key Features at Launch

### Open World

The continent of **Pywel** features five distinct regions at launch:

| Region | Level Range | Theme |
|--------|------------|-------|
| **Hernand** | 1-15 | Rolling hills, forests, starting area |
| **Lioncrest** | 10-20 | Noble estates, political intrigue |
| **Howling Hill** | 15-25 | Rugged frontier, pioneer settlements |
| **Northern Wasteland** | 20-30 | Desert, ancient ruins |
| **Pywel Central** | 25-40+ | Capital region, endgame territory |

Each region has its own **Contribution Points** system, unique enemies, hidden dungeons, and lore to discover.

### Combat System

- **Multiple weapon types**: One-handed swords, two-handed swords, dual blades, spears, halberds, bows, crossbows
- **Deep skill trees**: Keen Senses, Sword Mastery, Shield Arts, Wrestling, Archery, Abyss Powers
- **Posture system**: Break enemy guards with strategic attacks and parries
- **Elemental combat**: Fire, ice, lightning, Abyss, and Holy elements with strengths and weaknesses
- **Mounted combat**: Fight from horseback against certain enemies

### Story and Characters

- **60+ hours** of main story content
- **Kliff** as the protagonist — leader of the Greymane mercenaries
- The **Black Bears** as the primary antagonistic force
- The **Abyss** — a supernatural power central to the narrative
- Multiple **companion characters** with their own questlines and stories
- Branching dialogue options that affect companion relationships

### Dynamic Systems

- **Day/night cycle** affecting enemy spawns, NPC schedules, and quest availability
- **Weather system** influencing combat (rain weakens fire, storms boost lightning)
- **Reputation system** across different factions and regions
- **Camp management** with crafting, cooking, and companion interactions

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is there multiplayer?

Crimson Desert is a **single-player experience**. There is no online multiplayer or co-op at launch. Pearl Abyss has stated the game is designed from the ground up as a solo adventure.

### Is there a New Game+?

Yes. After completing the main story, New Game+ becomes available, carrying over your level, gear, and unlocked skills while scaling enemy difficulty.

### How long is the game?

- **Main story**: 60-70 hours
- **Main story + side content**: 100-120 hours
- **Completionist**: 150+ hours (all collectibles, hidden dungeons, max Contribution)

### Will there be mod support?

Pearl Abyss has not announced official mod support for PC, but the community is already exploring the game's file structure.

### Is the season pass required?

No. The base game is a complete experience. The season pass adds two story expansions that are entirely optional.
