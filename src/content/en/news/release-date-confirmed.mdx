## Official Release Date Announcement

Pearl Abyss has officially announced that Crimson Desert will launch worldwide. This is exciting news for all fans who have been waiting patiently.

### What We Know

- **Platforms**: PC, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S
- **Editions**: Standard, Deluxe, and Ultimate editions available for pre-order
- **Pre-order Bonuses**: Exclusive mount skin, weapon set, and 7-day early access

### Key Features at Launch

1. Massive open world spanning 5 distinct regions
2. Deep action combat system with multiple weapon types
3. Full single-player campaign with 60+ hours of story content
4. Dynamic weather and day/night cycle affecting gameplay

The game promises to deliver an experience unlike anything before in the action-RPG genre.
