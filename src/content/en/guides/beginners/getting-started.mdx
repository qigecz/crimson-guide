## Welcome to Crimson Desert

This guide covers the essential basics you need to know before diving into the world of Crimson Desert.

### Controls and Setup

- **Movement**: WASD keys / Left stick
- **Attack**: Left click / RT
- **Dodge**: Space / B button
- **Interact**: E / A button

### First Steps

When you first start the game, you'll find yourself in a brief tutorial area. Pay close attention to the combat mechanics — they'll be essential throughout your journey.

### Combat Fundamentals

Crimson Desert features a deep action combat system. Master these basics early:

1. **Light attacks** are fast but deal less damage
2. **Heavy attacks** are slower but can stagger enemies
3. **Dodging** has invincibility frames — time them well
4. **Parrying** opens enemies up for critical hits

### What to Do First

- Complete the tutorial section
- Explore the starting area thoroughly for hidden items
- Talk to NPCs for side quests
- Upgrade your weapon at the first blacksmith you encounter
