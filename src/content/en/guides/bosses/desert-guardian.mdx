## Desert Guardian Boss Guide

The Desert Guardian is the first major boss encounter in Crimson Desert. Here's how to take it down.

### Boss Overview

- **Location**: Northern Wasteland
- **Recommended Level**: 15+
- **Weakness**: Fire element attacks
- **Rewards**: Guardian's Shield, 500 Gold

### Attack Patterns

The Desert Guardian has three main attack phases:

1. **Phase 1 (100%-70% HP)**: Slow ground slams and sweeping arm attacks
2. **Phase 2 (70%-30% HP)**: Adds sandstorm AoE attacks, more aggressive
3. **Phase 3 (30%-0% HP)**: Enraged mode, faster attacks, new charge attack

### Strategy

- **Dodge right** when he raises his left arm for the ground slam
- Keep **fire weapons** equipped for bonus damage
- Use the **pillars** in the arena for cover during sandstorm attacks
- In Phase 3, parry his charge attack for a major opening

### Recommended Loadout

- Weapon: Ember Blade (fire damage)
- Armor: Lightweight for better dodge rolls
- Items: Fire Bombs ×5, Health Potions ×10
