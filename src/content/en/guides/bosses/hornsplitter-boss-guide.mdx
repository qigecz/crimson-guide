## Hornsplitter Boss Guide

Hornsplitter is considered one of the **first major difficulty spikes** in Crimson Desert. Many players get stuck here, but with the right preparation and strategy, it's very manageable.

### Boss Overview

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Location** | Karin Quarry (Hernand region) |
| **Recommended Level** | 12-15 |
| **Weakness** | Fire damage, side attacks |
| **Resistance** | Ice, frontal attacks |
| **Phases** | 3 phases |
| **Rewards** | Mining Knuckledrill, Contribution Points |

### Phase 1: 100% — 70% HP

Hornsplitter starts with slow but powerful **overhead slam attacks**:

- **Horn Charge** — runs straight at you with its horns
  - Dodge sideways (not backward)
- **Ground Slam** — rears up and smashes the ground
  - Dodge right, then punish the recovery window
- **Stomp Combo** — two quick stomps in succession
  - Block the first, dodge the second

> **Key timing:** After every Ground Slam, there's a 2-second window to land 3-4 hits on the side.

### Phase 2: 70% — 30% HP

Hornsplitter becomes more aggressive and adds:

- **AoE Earthquake** — damages in a radius around the boss
  - Run away when you see the ground crack animation
- **Double Charge** — charges twice in quick succession
  - Dodge the first, wait for the second, then punish
- **Throwing Rocks** — picks up and hurls boulders
  - Use pillars/cover to block

### Phase 3: 30% — 0% HP (Enraged)

The boss enters **enraged mode**:
- Glowing red eyes and faster attack speed
- New attack: **Spinning Charge** — rapid circular charge
- All existing attacks become faster

> **Critical:** Use your **Palmar Pill** at the START of Phase 3. If you die and restart, you want to save it for this phase.

## Strategy Summary

1. **Always position to the side** — frontal attacks bounce off
2. **Punish Ground Slam recovery** — your main damage window
3. **Save Palmar Pill for Phase 3** — don't waste it early
4. **Use Shield Charge to break super armor** — creates openings
5. **If struggling, go do side quests** — come back 2-3 levels higher

### If You're Still Struggling

- Complete the **Estate in Dismay** questline for better gear
- Farm Abyss Artifacts to allocate to your stats
- Unlock **Keen Senses II** for the Perfect Dodge
- Cook food for attack/defense buffs before the fight
