## Weapon Types and Upgrades Guide

Understanding the weapon system is crucial for mastering Crimson Desert's combat.

### Weapon Types

#### Swords
- **One-Handed Swords**: Fast attacks, can equip shield
- **Two-Handed Swords**: High damage, slower swings
- **Dual Blades**: Fastest attack speed, lower per-hit damage

#### Polearms
- **Spears**: Long reach, good for keeping distance
- **Halberds**: Versatile with both thrusting and sweeping attacks

#### Ranged
- **Bows**: Silent ranged attacks, various arrow types
- **Crossbows**: Higher damage, slower reload

### Upgrade System

Weapons can be upgraded at any blacksmith using:

| Material | Source | Rarity |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Iron Ore | Mining nodes | Common |
| Steel Ingot | Blacksmith forge | Uncommon |
| Moonstone | Boss drops | Rare |
| Dragon Scale | End-game bosses | Legendary |

### Tips

- Always upgrade your main weapon first
- Element infusions can be changed at enchantment stations
- Some weapons have hidden upgrade paths unlocked by special quests
