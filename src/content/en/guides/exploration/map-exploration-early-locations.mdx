## Why Explore Early?

Crimson Desert rewards exploration heavily. Many of the best early-game items are found in **hidden locations** that don't require combat to reach. Some areas are actually easier to visit early because enemies only spawn once you activate the related questline.

## Fast Travel System

Unlocking fast travel points should be your **first priority** when entering any new region.

### How Fast Travel Works

- Fast travel points are tied to **Abyss Nexus** locations
- They appear where **Mysterious Energies** are found
- Each region has multiple travel points
- Some quest objectives require frequent back-and-forth travel

> **Always activate Abyss Nexus points** when you see them. Even if you don't need them now, you will later.

## Exploration Tools

### Lantern / Blinding Flash

Use **Blinding Flash** or the **Lantern** to highlight nearby points of interest:
- Hidden collectibles
- Puzzle solution clues
- Secret passages
- Loot you might have missed

### Mysterious Energies

These glowing spots appear throughout the world and indicate:
- Nearby fast travel points
- Hidden dungeons or caves
- Abyss Artifacts
- Lore items

## Clearing Fog of War

Each region has a **fog of war** that hides unexplored areas. To clear it:

1. **Walk through areas** — proximity reveals the map
2. **Use high vantage points** — some towers reveal large sections
3. **Complete quests** — many quest paths reveal map areas naturally
4. **Find all Bells** — ringing bells clears fog in a radius

> Aim to clear at least 60% of each region's map. The remaining 40% usually contains end-game content.
