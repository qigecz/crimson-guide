## Chapter 2: Golden Greed

Chapter 2 is where Crimson Desert truly opens up. After the events of the prologue and Chapter 1 in Hernand, Kliff's pursuit of the Black Bears leads him deeper into the continent of Pywel. This chapter introduces **Lioncrest** — a region defined by wealth, political corruption, and the ever-present threat of the Abyss.

Golden Greed is roughly 6-8 hours of story content, but with side quests and exploration, expect to spend 12-15 hours in this region.

### Prerequisites

Before starting Chapter 2, ensure you've completed:

- [x] All Prologue objectives
- [x] Chapter 1 main quests (Trials of Kindness, Traces)
- [x] Recommended level: **8-10** (enemies start at level 10 in Lioncrest)
- [x] Hwando or equivalent weapon at +3 or higher
- [x] At least one piece of the Fallen Kingdom armor set

> **Tip:** If you're level 7 or below, complete side quests in Hernand first. The difficulty jump between Chapter 1 and Chapter 2 is significant.

## Entering Lioncrest

### The Road to Lioncrest Manor

The chapter begins with Kliff traveling the main road toward **Lioncrest Manor**, the seat of power in the region. Along the way, you'll encounter:

1. **Ambush by Black Bear scouts** — a group of 4-5 enemies. This is your first taste of the chapter's combat difficulty.
2. **A wounded merchant** — talk to him for lore about the region and a free Health Potion.
3. **First sight of Lioncrest Manor** — a cinematic introduces the region's grandeur and underlying tension.

### First NPC Encounters

Upon arriving at Lioncrest town, speak with these key NPCs in order:

| NPC | Location | Purpose |
|-----|----------|---------|
| **Magistrate Hale** | Town Hall | Main quest giver for the chapter |
| **Innkeeper Rhea** | The Gilded Flask inn | Rests, saves, and sells food recipes |
| **Blacksmith Torban** | Market district | Weapon upgrades and repairs |
| **Scholar Eveline** | Library annex | Lore about the Abyss and side quests |

## Main Quest: Golden Chains

### Objective 1: Investigate the Merchant District

Magistrate Hale asks Kliff to investigate reports of Black Bear activity in the merchant district. This section is part exploration, part investigation.

**Steps:**

1. Head to the **merchant district** (southeast of town center)
2. Use **Keen Senses** (if unlocked) to highlight clues — look for scratch marks on doors and disturbed crates
3. Find **three clues** scattered through the district:
   - A Black Bear insignia dropped behind a spice stall
   - A torn letter referencing "the shipment" near the warehouse
   - An eyewitness account from a terrified merchant (speak to him at the fish market)
4. Return to Magistrate Hale with your findings

> **Tip:** While investigating, explore the upper floors of the warehouses. One contains a chest with the **Lioncrest Blade** — a solid one-handed sword with lightning infusion.

### Objective 2: The Warehouse Raid

Hale sends you to raid the suspected Black Bear warehouse at the docks.

**Enemy Composition:**

| Enemy Type | Count | Level | Notes |
|------------|-------|-------|-------|
| Black Bear Thugs | 4 | 10 | Standard enemies, attack in pairs |
| Black Bear Archer | 2 | 10 | Elevated positions, use cover |
| Black Bear Enforcer | 1 | 12 | Mini-boss, heavy armor, uses a mace |

**Strategy:**

1. Approach from the **rooftops** (climb the crates on the east side) to take out the archers first
2. Drop down and engage the thugs — they attack in pairs but are vulnerable to grabs and throws
3. Save the Enforcer for last. He has super armor on most attacks, so use **Shield Charge** to break his guard, then punish with a combo
4. After clearing enemies, search the warehouse for the **shipment manifest** — a key story item

**Loot in the Warehouse:**

- Shipment Manifest (key item)
- Silver Pouch (45 Silver)
- Fire Bombs x3
- Steel Ingot x2

### Objective 3: Report and Consequence

Return to Hale. A story cutscene plays revealing that the Black Bears are smuggling **Abyss-tainted artifacts** through Lioncrest. This connects to the supernatural events Kliff experienced in the prologue's Unknown Space.

Hale asks you to meet an informant at the **old chapel** outside town.

## Main Quest: The Gilded Cage

### The Chapel Meeting

Travel to the old chapel (follow the quest marker). A short exploration sequence through the overgrown graveyard leads to the informant — a former Black Bear member named **Darin**.

**Darin reveals:**

- The Black Bears are operating under a leader called **The Golden Hand**
- The Golden Hand has been purchasing Abyss artifacts and experimenting with them
- A major deal is happening at the **Lioncrest underground vaults** in two days (in-game time)

> **Key Choice:** Darin asks if you'll work with him or go alone. Working with Darin gives you a companion for the vault section (he fights alongside you). Going alone gives more XP but the vault enemies are harder.

### Preparing for the Vault

Before the vault mission, take time to prepare:

1. **Upgrade your weapon** to at least +5 at Torban's blacksmith
2. **Stock up on potions** — bring at least 10 Health Potions and 3 Stamina Tonics
3. **Cook Grilled Meat** for the attack buff
4. **Complete available side quests** (see side quest section below) for additional XP and gear
5. **Unlock Shield Charge** if you haven't — the vault has several armored enemies

## Main Quest: Into the Vault

This is the chapter's main dungeon sequence. The Lioncrest underground vault is a multi-level area with combat encounters, puzzles, and a boss fight.

### Vault Level 1: The Banking Halls

**Enemies:** Black Bear guards, Abyss-corrupted rats

**Key encounters:**

- **Guard Room**: 4 Black Bear guards. Use the chandeliers (shoot them down with arrows) for environmental damage.
- **Corrupted Rat Swarm**: These Abyss-tainted rats are fast but fragile. Use AoE attacks or Fire Bombs.
- **Puzzle Door**: Align three rotating tiles to match the pattern on the nearby wall fresco. The solution is: **Shield, Sword, Crown** (left to right).

### Vault Level 2: The Archives

**Enemies:** Black Bear officers, Abyss Phantoms

This level introduces **Abyss Phantoms** — spectral enemies that are resistant to physical damage but weak to Holy and Abyss-element attacks.

**Key encounters:**

- **Archive Guardians**: Two Black Bear officers with halberds. They coordinate attacks — focus one down first.
- **Phantom Hallway**: A corridor filled with Abyss Phantoms. Use Holy-infused arrows or Abyss Powers to clear them efficiently.
- **Lore Room**: Contains important story documents about the Golden Hand's plans. Read all documents for full context.

> **Tip:** The Archives contain a hidden room behind a bookshelf (look for the one with a scratched floor — push it). Inside: **Moonstone x1** and a lore entry about the original Pywel civilization.

### Vault Level 3: The Golden Chamber (Boss Fight)

The deepest level holds the **Golden Hand** himself, along with his prize — a massive Abyss artifact.

## Boss Fight: The Golden Hand

The Golden Hand is Chapter 2's primary boss encounter. He fights with dual enchanted daggers and Abyss powers.

### Boss Overview

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Name** | The Golden Hand (real name: Varen Kost) |
| **Level** | 14 |
| **Weakness** | Fire, heavy attacks |
| **Resistance** | Abyss element, quick attacks |
| **Phases** | 2 phases |
| **Companion** | Darin (if you chose to work together) |

### Phase 1: Dagger Fighter (100%-50% HP)

Varen uses fast dual-dagger combos with Abyss-enhanced slashes:

| Attack | Telegraph | Counter |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| **Shadow Strike** | Blue glow on daggers (1 sec) | Dodge backward, then punish |
| **Dagger Flurry** | Steps forward aggressively | Parry the first hit, punish the rest |
| **Abyss Slash** | Purple energy wave | Roll through it (i-frames) |
| **Vanish** | Disappears in smoke | Watch for dust particles, dodge when he reappears behind you |

**Strategy:** Varen is fast but fragile. **Parry his Dagger Flurry** for the biggest openings. Use heavy attacks when you get a window — he staggers easily from posture damage.

### Phase 2: Abyss Empowered (50%-0% HP)

Varen channels the Abyss artifact, gaining new attacks:

- **Abyss Pulse** — AoE pulse around him, damages in a 5-meter radius
- **Shadow Clones** — creates 2 illusions that deal reduced damage
- **Gold Drain** — steals Silver from your inventory (minor, but annoying)
- **Empowered Vanish** — faster and he strikes three times on reappearance

**Strategy:** In Phase 2, identify the real Varen by watching for the one with a shadow (clones have no shadows). Save your Abyss Power for this phase and use it for burst damage when he's recovering from Abyss Pulse.

### Rewards

- **Golden Daggers** (unique dual blade weapon)
- **Abyss Artifact Shard** (key item for story progression)
- **300 Gold**
- **Lore Entry: The Golden Hand's Ledger**

## Lore Discoveries in Chapter 2

Throughout Lioncrest, you'll find lore entries that expand the world:

- **The Fall of the Golden Court**: How Lioncrest's nobility lost power to the merchant class
- **Abyss Manifestations**: Scholarly notes on how the Abyss interacts with the physical world
- **Black Bear Origins**: Hints about where the Black Bears came from and their true goals
- **The Greymane Legacy**: Kliff's own history, connected to events in Lioncrest

## Transition to Chapter 3

After defeating The Golden Hand, a cutscene plays showing Kliff examining the Abyss artifact. It triggers a vision of **Howling Hill** and a powerful Abyss entity. Darin (if present) provides intelligence about Black Bear movements to the north.

Chapter 3 will take you to **Howling Hill**, the rugged frontier region. You should be around **level 14-16** before proceeding.

### End-of-Chapter Checklist

Before moving to Chapter 3:

- [ ] Defeated The Golden Hand
- [ ] Completed all available side quests in Lioncrest
- [ ] Upgraded main weapon to +5 or higher
- [ ] Unlocked Shield Charge and Keen Senses II
- [ ] Collected all lore entries in the vault
- [ ] Visited the Lioncrest Manor Gold Bar location (see [Exploration Guide](/en/guides/exploration/map-exploration-early-locations))
- [ ] Restocked on potions and crafting materials

> **Tip:** Talk to all NPCs again after completing the vault. Many have new dialogue reflecting the story changes, and some offer additional quests or items.
