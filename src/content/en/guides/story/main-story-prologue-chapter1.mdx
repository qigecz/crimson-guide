## Prologue: Dead of Night

The story of Crimson Desert begins with **Kliff**, the leader of the Greymane mercenary group. Your camp is suddenly invaded by the Black Bears, setting the stage for an epic journey across the continent of Pywel.

### Ambush

The opening sequence serves as both a story introduction and a combat tutorial. You'll learn:

- **Basic combat mechanics** — light attacks, heavy attacks, and blocking
- **Parry timing** — crucial for surviving later boss encounters
- **Movement and dodging** — essential for navigating the chaotic battlefield

> **Tip:** Pay close attention to the parry tutorial. This mechanic is the foundation of Crimson Desert's combat system and will be tested repeatedly throughout the story.

### Unknown Space

After the ambush, Kliff is pulled into a mysterious dimension. This section introduces:

- The **Abyss** — a supernatural force central to the game's lore
- Basic **exploration mechanics** — using your lantern to find hidden paths
- First encounter with **Abyss Artifacts** —_collectible items that grant stat bonuses

### Key Choices in the Prologue

The prologue is largely linear, but keep these in mind:

1. **Talk to all Greymane members** before leaving camp — some unlock later side quests
2. **Collect all loot** during the escape sequence — you can't return later
3. **Don't skip the combat tutorials** — you'll regret it in Chapter 2

## Chapter 1: The First Encounter

After the prologue, Kliff arrives in the region of **Hernand**, marking the true beginning of the open-world adventure.

### Trials of Kindness

Your first main quest introduces you to the locals and the region's troubled state.

**Objectives:**
- Help the villagers with basic tasks
- Learn the **Contribution Points** system
- Unlock your first **camp features**

> **Tip:** Completing all optional objectives here gives you a head start on Hernand's Contribution rewards, which include valuable armor pieces.

### Traces

This quest kicks off your investigation into the Black Bears and the mysterious forces at play.

**Sub Quests available:**
- **Polar Opposites** — introduces the element system
- **Abyss Without Balance** — your first real Abyss puzzle encounter

### Early Exploration Checklist

Before moving to Chapter 2, make sure you've done these:

| Priority | Task | Reward |
|----------|------|--------|
| ★★★ | Unlock fast travel points | Easier traversal |
| ★★★ | Visit Sanctum of Temperance | Odeck's Protector Plate Boots |
| ★★☆ | Obtain the Hwando weapon | Strong early-game sword |
| ★★☆ | Get a pet for auto-looting | Quality of life upgrade |
| ★☆☆ | Rob Hernand Bank (optional) | 40+ Silver |

### Recommended Level for Chapter 2

You should be around **level 8-10** before advancing to Chapter 2: Golden Greed. If the enemies feel too difficult, complete side quests and bounties to earn Contribution Points and better gear.
